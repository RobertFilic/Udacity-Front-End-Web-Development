Clownfish

A layer of mucus clownfish's skin makes it immune to the fish-eating anemone's lethal sting.

  • Scientific Name: Amphiprioninae
  • Average length: 11 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 7 years
  • Havital: Tropical coral reef

Bright orange with three distinctive white bars, clown anemonefish are among the most recognised of all reefdwellers. Ther reach about 11 cenitmeters in length, and are named for the multicolored sea anemone in which they make their homes.
